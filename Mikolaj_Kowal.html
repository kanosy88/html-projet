Location and Culture

Poland is a country located in Central Europe, in the northeast of the continent. It shares borders with Germany, the Czech Republic, Slovakia, Ukraine, Belarus, and Lithuania, and has direct access to the Baltic Sea in the north. Poland is a modern country, rich in history and traditions, with a strong culture, a population of about 38 million people, an area of 312,000 km², a unique language (Polish), and a local currency called the złoty.

    The 10 Activities to Do

  • Discover Krakow and its historic center
  • Visit the Wieliczka Salt Mines
  • Explore Warsaw, the modern capital
  • Stroll through the old town of Gdansk
  • Hike in the Tatra Mountains
  • Explore the medieval castle of Malbork
  • Taste the famous Polish pierogi
  • Discover Wroclaw and its small dwarf statues
  • Enjoy Poznan's nightlife
  • Visit Torun, the city of gingerbread and Copernicus

Description

Morocco is a North African country located between the Atlantic Ocean and the Mediterranean Sea. It is distinguished by the diversity of its landscapes, ranging from beaches to the Atlas Mountains and the Sahara Desert. Rich in ancient history, Morocco possesses an incredible culture. Its traditions are reflected in its crafts, architecture, and cuisine. Hospitality and a spirit of sharing play an important role in daily life. Today, Morocco is a country that blends tradition and modernity, with a developing economy and a strong cultural identity.

Population

Morocco has approximately 37 million inhabitants, making it one of the most populated countries in North Africa.
